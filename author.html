About the author

I'm a media enthusiast and a freelance front-end developer with a strong connection to the beautiful city of Lund, where I've resided with my partner for the past four years. In the spring of 2022, I was captivated by the charm of Adelgatan and felt compelled to create this website, both as a tribute to this ancient street and as a canvas to showcase my proficiency in HTML and CSS programming.

In 2023, I earned my master's degree in media and communication studies from Lund University, and I'm eager to channel my passion for web UI/UX and visual media into my future career. My journey into web development began during my tenure at Fosun Pharma's media and communication department, where I designed captivating layouts for WeChat mobile news. While using the 'xiumi' software to assemble visually appealing web pages, I recognized the need for more personalized elements. This sparked my journey into adding hand-drawn illustrations to web pages and eventually delving into self-teaching front-end development.

Today, I am proficient in front-end programming languages, enabling me to pursue my dream career. I'm drawn to web development because it represents one of the most influential and dynamic forms of visual media in the modern world. I'm excited to continue crafting engaging and user-friendly digital experiences.

2023 tog jag stolt min masterexamen i medie- och kommunikationsvetenskap från Lunds universitet, och jag är sugen på att kanalisera min passion för webb-UI/UX och visuella medier in i min framtida karriär. Idag är jag skicklig i front-end programmeringsspråk, vilket gör att jag kan fortsätta min drömkarriär. Jag dras till webbutveckling eftersom det representerar en av de mest inflytelserika och dynamiska formerna av visuella medier i den moderna världen. Jag är glad över att fortsätta skapa engagerande och användarvänliga digitala upplevelser.
